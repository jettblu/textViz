Problution

First World Problem

You have a phone. You have text messages. A lot of text messages. You have questions about those text messages.

First World Solution

TextViz stands on the shoulders of Guido Van Rossum and basic Machine Learning principles as it strives to reach an understanding of your texts. All for you.

Server Side Problem

You may experience slightly longer page load times than you're used to, especially if you have a lot of texts. If you have any complaints contact the folks who made the internet.

Terminology

Term
Meaning

Tokens

Pieces of text that are fed into TextViz's machine learning model.

Confidence

How sure the TextViz model is that your text is positive or negative.

Word Count

Just what it implies: the number of words in a text or group of texts.

Lag

The time between sending and receiving a text.
